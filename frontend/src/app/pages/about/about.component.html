About LiDa Software

LiDa Software started as a small side project by Hen Faibish, a developer who just couldn't stop building things, even on weekends. It's not a company with investors, slogans, or slides full of buzzwords. It's just one guy who really loves code and believes good ideas should be shared, not locked behind paywalls or jargon.

LiDa creates small, useful, sometimes weird tools... the kind that make developers smile, save time, or say "oh, that's actually clever." No hype. No AI drama. Just clean code, curiosity, and the joy of making things work (eventually).

When Hen isn't coding, he's probably testing another idea, writing about software, or explaining to his kids why "just five more minutes" of debugging can actually mean an hour.

psychology Virtual Team Kit AI-Powered Project Management

Virtual Team Kit is LiDa's answer to the complexity of modern project management. Instead of juggling multiple tools and endless meetings, we built a platform that uses AI to help you plan, organize, and execute projects with virtual teams.

What Makes It Special:

  • check_circle AI Command Center - Generate documentation, sprint plans, and RACI charts instantly
  • check_circle Virtual Teams - Build diverse teams with predefined roles and skills
  • check_circle Smart Planning - AI-assisted sprint planning and task generation
  • check_circle GitHub Integration - Native connection to your development workflow
  • check_circle Export Everything - Multiple formats (Markdown, JSON, PDF)
