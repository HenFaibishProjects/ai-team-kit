quiz Frequently Asked Questions How do I create a virtual team?

Navigate to Team Management, click "Add Team Member", and fill in the details including role, skills, and experience level. You can choose from predefined roles or create custom ones.

 What AI models does the system use?

Virtual Team Kit uses advanced language models to generate documentation, sprint plans, and recommendations. You can customize AI prompts in the AI Command Center to fit your specific needs.

 Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export projects, sprint plans, RACI charts, and ADRs in multiple formats including Markdown, JSON, and PDF. Look for the export button on any page.

 How does GitHub integration work?

Connect your GitHub account in Settings. Once connected, you can create repositories, manage issues, and track pull requests directly from Virtual Team Kit.

 Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored securely and encrypted. We follow industry best practices for data protection and privacy.
