Adding Team Members

Learn how to build and manage your virtual team effectively

info Overview

The Virtual Team Kit allows you to create and manage virtual team members with specific roles, skills, and expertise. These AI-powered team members can help you plan, execute, and manage your projects effectively.

lightbulb
Key Benefit: Build a complete team without hiring constraints. Add specialists exactly when you need them, with the precise skills your project requires.
list_alt Step-by-Step Guide Navigate to Team Management

How to get there:

  1. From the Dashboard, click on "Team Management" in the navigation
  2. Or use the direct link in the main menu
tips_and_updates

You can also access team management from within any project by clicking the team icon.

 Click "Add Team Member"

What you'll see:

  • A button labeled "Add Team Member" or "+ New Member"
  • Your existing team members (if any) displayed in cards

Click the button to open the team member creation form.

 Fill in Basic Information

Required fields:

badge
Name: Give your team member a professional name (e.g., "Sarah Chen", "Alex Rodriguez")
work
Role: Define their primary role (e.g., "Senior Developer", "UX Designer", "Project Manager")
email
Email: Provide a unique email address for identification
Define Skills & Expertise

Add relevant skills:

  • Technical Skills: Programming languages, frameworks, tools
  • Domain Expertise: Industry knowledge, specialized areas
  • Soft Skills: Leadership, communication, problem-solving
Example for a Frontend Developer:
React TypeScript CSS/SASS UI/UX Design Responsive Design
Set Experience Level

Choose the appropriate level:

school

Junior (0-2 years)

Learning fundamentals, needs guidance

work

Mid-Level (2-5 years)

Independent worker, solid skills

star

Senior (5+ years)

Expert, can lead and mentor

 Save & Assign to Projects

Final steps:

  1. Review all information for accuracy
  2. Click "Save" or "Create Team Member"
  3. Assign them to relevant projects immediately or later

 help_outline Common Questions How many team members should I add?

It depends on your project scope:

  • Small projects: 3-5 team members
  • Medium projects: 6-10 team members
  • Large projects: 10+ team members

Focus on quality over quantity. A well-defined small team is better than a large unfocused one.

 Can I use the same team member across multiple projects?

Yes! Team members can be assigned to multiple projects. This is useful for:

  • Specialists who contribute to multiple initiatives
  • Leadership roles that oversee several projects
  • Shared resources across related projects
 What if I need to change a team member's role?

You can edit team members at any time:

  1. Go to Team Management
  2. Find the team member
  3. Click "Edit" or the pencil icon
  4. Update their information
  5. Save changes

Changes will be reflected across all projects they're assigned to.

 Can I delete team members?

Yes, but be careful:

  • Deleting removes them from all projects
  • Historical data may be affected
  • Consider "archiving" instead if they might be needed later
warning

Always review project assignments before deleting a team member.
