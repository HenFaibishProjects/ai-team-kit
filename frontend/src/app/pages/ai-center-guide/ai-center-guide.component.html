help_outline How to Use the AI Command Center search Finding the Right Template

Search Methods:

  • Search Bar: Type keywords related to your task (e.g., "sprint", "security", "testing")
  • Category Filter: Click on category chips to view templates in specific domains
  • Difficulty Filter: Select Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced based on complexity
  • Browse All: Scroll through the grid to explore all available templates

Template Information:

Each template card shows:

  • Title and description
  • Difficulty level badge
  • Category tag
  • Related tags for better searchability
  • Required input variables
 edit Using Templates

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Select Template: Click "Use This Template" on any template card
  2. Form Opens: The prompt builder form will appear with the template loaded
  3. Fill Details: Complete the form sections:
    • Project Information: Name, technology stack, team size, timeframe
    • Additional Context: Budget, deadline, stakeholders, constraints
    • Problem & Goal: Current issue, desired outcome, specific goals
  4. Generate: Click "Generate from Template" to create your customized prompt
  5. Review: Check the generated prompt in the preview area
  6. Copy: Click "Copy to Clipboard" to copy the prompt
lightbulb

Pro Tip: The more details you provide, the more specific and useful the AI's response will be!

 create Creating Custom Prompts

Custom Prompt Builder:

  1. Open Builder: Click the floating "Custom Prompt Builder" button (bottom-right)
  2. Choose Method:
    • Template-Based: Fill in project details and let the system generate a structured prompt
    • From Scratch: Write your own prompt in the "Custom Query" field
  3. Add Context: Select appropriate context (General, Sprint, Feature, etc.)
  4. Generate: Click "Create Custom Prompt"
  5. Copy & Use: Copy the generated prompt to your clipboard
lightbulb

Pro Tip: Even when writing custom prompts, filling in the project details adds valuable context automatically!

 psychology Using with AI Assistants

Compatible AI Tools:

  • ChatGPT (OpenAI) - chat.openai.com
  • Claude (Anthropic) - claude.ai
  • Gemini (Google) - gemini.google.com
  • Copilot (Microsoft) - copilot.microsoft.com
  • Any other AI chat interface

Best Practices:

  1. Copy the Prompt: Use the "Copy to Clipboard" button
  2. Open AI Tool: Navigate to your preferred AI assistant
  3. Paste & Send: Paste the prompt and send it
  4. Review Response: Carefully read the AI's response
  5. Ask Follow-ups: Don't hesitate to ask clarifying questions or request modifications
  6. Iterate: Refine the output through conversation until you get exactly what you need
warning

Important: Always review and validate AI-generated content before implementing it in production!

 tips_and_updates Pro Tips & Best Practices

Maximizing Prompt Effectiveness:

  • Be Specific: Provide detailed information about your project, technology, and goals
  • Use Context: Select the appropriate context to frame your question correctly
  • Combine Templates: Use multiple templates in sequence for complex, multi-faceted problems
  • Iterate: Don't settle for the first response - ask follow-up questions
  • Save Successful Prompts: Keep a library of prompts that worked well for future reference
  • Share with Team: Share effective prompts with your team members
  • Customize: Modify templates to better fit your specific needs
  • Validate Output: Always review AI-generated content critically

Common Pitfalls to Avoid:

  • ❌ Being too vague or generic
  • ❌ Skipping important context fields
  • ❌ Accepting the first response without iteration
  • ❌ Not validating AI-generated code or advice
  • ❌ Using prompts without understanding the output

 question_answer Frequently Asked Questions Do I need to fill in all form fields?

No, but the more information you provide, the better the AI's response will be. Required fields are marked, but optional fields add valuable context.

 Can I modify the generated prompts?

Absolutely! The generated prompts are starting points. Feel free to edit them before copying to your AI assistant.

 Which AI assistant works best?

All major AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot) work well. Choose based on your preference and subscription. Different AIs may provide slightly different perspectives, which can be valuable.

 Can I save my prompts?

Currently, prompts are generated on-demand. We recommend copying successful prompts to a personal knowledge base or documentation system for future reference.

 Are the templates updated?

Yes! We regularly review and update templates based on industry best practices and user feedback. New templates are added periodically.

 Can I request new templates?

Yes! We welcome suggestions for new templates. Contact your team lead or submit feedback through the Help & Support menu.
